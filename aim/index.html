We Aim to . . .

● To set up a Center of Indian Cultural Heritage and Excellence in Kozhikode and stretch out its exercises to all over India for the time being, and to deliver magnanimous works to the penniless fragment of the general public.


● To Discover, Nurture and Admire the abilities of the general public, particularly young people.


● To set up and keep up with parks, gardens, gyms, sports clubs, and rest houses, for of public.

● To build up and keep up with homes for the matured, orphanages or different foundations for the alleviation and help to the defenceless and down and out, vagrants, widows and aged persons

● To set up and foster establishments for the genuinely debilitated and handicapped or intellectually impeded people and to give them schooling, food, clothing and such other assistance.

● To give alleviation and help to the poor during natural calamities like famine, earthquake, flood, fire, epidemic, and so forth and to give gifts and other help to organizations, foundations or people occupied with such alleviation work.

● To establish and promote participatory tourism in and around of each cultural centre

● To establish and develop entrepreneurial culture and enterprises among the public, especially in and around of each cultural heritage centre.

● To build up an audio-visual media institute and extend training and production services.

● To establish and promote health tourism, martial arts, yoga science etc…

● To establish organic farming, dairy farm units and nursery

● To build up various school of cultural arts like Indian classical, traditional, western and contemporary.

● To establish and affiliate similar organizations to cope up with the objectives

● To arrange far-reaching cultural events and make recipes of Indian culture as traditional dance forms, choreography, theatre arts, folklore, ancestral social structures, tribal cultural forms and so on all through India.

● To introduce social gala all around the world as the time being with the official invitation from other nations/ organizations.

● Arrangement and promote parallel education institutions, giving most importance to Arts and Culture.

● To initiate and promote cultivating exercises among students
